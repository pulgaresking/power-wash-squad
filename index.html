Hidden Benefits of Exterior Pressure Washing Services

Pressure washing an Ann Arbor MI home’s exterior can remove insect nests, including termite tunnels built around a home’s foundation, to help avoid an infestation in the home. Cleaning a deck of harmful chemicals and irritants means cleaner air for your entire family, including your pets!

Soft washing around pool fences removes chlorine and other damaging chemical residues, keeping those fences protected from rotting away. Washing away mold, mildew, and algae from fences, decks, and other timber surfaces also protects that wood from getting soft and then cracking and chipping.
