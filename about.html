Protect Your Home or Commercial Business with “Soft Wash” Pressure Washing

Our “Soft Wash” pressure washing services do more than standard high-pressure washing services to just make a home or commercial business look good. When Power Wash Squad wash your house or building with a Soft Wash system, you remove damaging silt, grit, acid rain residue, residue from snow-clearing salt, pesticides, and fertilizers without damaging your home or landscape. Our soft wash cleaning method gently yet effectively removes debris that will eat away at a home’s brick or aluminum siding, causing cracks and leaks.

The soft wash power washing services offered by Power Wash Squad start with the application of high-quality but gentle detergents and surfactants. These cleansers dissolve layers of corrosive chemicals, drying dust, grime, and mud, without harming a structure’s brickwork or siding.

Why Choose a Soft Wash Home Exterior or Roof Cleaning Company?

The best pressure washing company is one that is concerned about the long-term condition of your roof, siding or other surfaces, and not just its immediate cleanliness. At Power Wash Squad, our soft wash roof cleaning and exterior washing system will gently wash away layers of dirt, soot, grime, and mud, as well as acid rain residue and water stains. A soft wash clean will restore the color of your roof, protecting your property’s curb appeal.

The surfactants used by Power Wash Squad Power Washing for soft wash roof cleaning will also completely kill mold spores, algae, moss, and mildew. Killing these spores ensures that this harmful vegetation won’t grow under roofing tiles and shingles and that they won’t damage your residential or commercial roof.
